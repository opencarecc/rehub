This website illustrates the experience and the progress of ReHub within the opencare Maker in Residence, which takes place in WeMake.

Our core values

ReHub project is an online platform and open source kit that allow the monitoring of fingers and hand movement for athletes, rehabilitation patients and music instrument students that needs a certain and digital data to monitor the exercise. Moreover, we are looking for our final user(s), who will try our product and help us develop different options:

  • Sport
  • Gaming
  • Educational
  • Medical
We want to built a community and start a business strategy.
